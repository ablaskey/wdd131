Bread Bowls

Ingredients

2 packets (4 1/2 tsp) Active Dry Yeast

2 1/4 c (540ml) warm Water (110℉-115℉)

2 tsp Sugar

2 tsp Salt

2 Tbsp Olive Oil

6 c Bread Flour

1.5 Tbsp Garlic Powder

1.5 Tbsp Italian Seasoning

1.5 Tbsp Rosemary

Egg Wash: 1 large Egg beaten with 1 Tbsp Water or Milk

Instructions

1. Pour warm Water over Yeast in the bowl of a stand mixer with dough hook attachment. Whisk together and allow to sit for 5 minutes, until mixture is frothy and foamy.

2. While mixing on low, add Sugar, Salt, Olive Oil, 4 c Bread Flour, and seasonings. Beat on low speed for 1 minute, then add remaining 2 cups of flour.

3. Knead the dough in the mixer, beating for 5-6 minutes. Add flour 1 tsp at a time as needed to acheive a soft, slightly tacky dough.

4. Form into ball and place into a large greased bowl, turning to grease top of bowl. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and place in warm environment to rise until doubled in size, about 90 minutes.

5. Punch down dough to release air bubbles. Remove dough from bowl, turning out onto lightly floured surface.

6. Cut into 6 even pieces. Form each into a ball.

7. Line 2 large baking pans with parchment paper. Place 3 dough balls on each, cover lightly, and set aside for 20 minutes to rest.

8. Preheat oven to 400℉. Brush each dough ball with Egg Wash, and score an X into the tops of each with a sharp knife.

9. Bake for 30 minutes or until golden brown. Bread bowls are done when center is 195℉

10. Let cool. Cut a large round out of the top and scoop out the center to fill with soup.
