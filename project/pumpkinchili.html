Pumpkin Chili

Ingredients

1 can 26 oz Tomato Sauce

1 can 27 oz Diced Tomatoes

2 cans Kidney or Black Beans, drained and rinsed

2 lbs Spicy Sausage (or 1 spicy and 1 regular)

1 medium Red Onion, diced

1-2 minced Garlic

1 each- Red, Yellow, and Green Bell Peppers, diced

1 can Corn, drained and rinsed (can also use bag of frozen corn)

1 can Green Chilies (opt)

2 Tbsp Chili Powder

8 oz Pumpkin Puree

Cinnamon

Instructions

1. In large pot, brown the sausage with onions and garlic over med heat, until meat is cooked through and onions are soft.

2. Add Tomato Sauce, Diced Tomatoes, Beans, and Corn to the meat mixture. Combine.

3. Add Bell Peppers, Chili Powder, Pumpkin, and a few shakes of Cinnamon and mix it up. Simmer until Peppers are tender.

4. Serve with sour cream, chives, and cheese if desired.
