Orange Chicken

Ingredients

2 lb Chicken Thighs, boneless and skinless, cut into 1" pieces

1 Egg

1 1/2 tsp Salt

1 pinch Pepper

2 Tbsp Vegetable Oil

1/2 c Cornstarch

1/4 c Flour

Sauce: 1 Tbsp Cornstarch

2 Tbsp Rice Wine

1/4 c Water

1 tsp Sesame Oil

3 Tbsp Soy Sauce

10 Tbsp Sugar

10 Tbsp White Vinegar

Zest of 1 Orange

To Finish: 1 1/2 Tbsp Ginger, minced

2 tsp Garlic, minced

1/2 tsp hot Red Chili Pepper, crushed

Instructions

1. Combine sauce ingredients and set aside.

2. Whisk together Egg, Salt, Pepper, and 1 Tbsp Oil in a bowl to coat chicken.

3. In separate bowl, add Cornstarch and Flour. Mix well.

4. Heat oil in large pan to 375℉

5. Dip chicken in egg mixture, then dredge in flour mixture.

6. Fry chicken for 3-4 minutes or until golden and crisp. Transfer to cooling rack and repeat with remaining chicken.

7. Drain most of the oil, leaving about 1 Tbsp. Add Ginger, Garlic, and Red Chili Peppers, cooking for about 10 seconds.

8. Add orange sauce that was combined in step 1, and bring to a boil.

9. Turn off heat and add cooked chicken, stirring until chicken is well coated.

Serve with rice.
