Chicken Gnocchi Soup

Ingredients

1 Tbsp Olive Oil

1/4 c Butter

1/4 c Flour

1/2 c Celery, finely diced

1 c Onion, finely diced

2 Garlic cloves, minced

4 c Half-and-Half

1(12 oz) pkg Gnocchi

1 c Carrots, finely shredded

1 c Chicken Breast, cooked and diced (or shredded)

2(14 oz) cans Chicken Broth

1 c Spinach, coarsely chopped

1/2 tsp Salt

1/2 tsp dried Thyme

1/2 tsp dried Parsley

1/4 tsp grated Nutmeg(opt)

Instructions

1. Heat Olive Oil and Butter in a large saucepan over med heat. Add Onion, Celery and Garlic. Saute until Onion is translucent.

2.Whisk in flour to make a roux. Let cook, while constantly stirring for about a minute, and then stir in Half-and-Half.

3.Cook the Gnocchi per package directions and set aside.

4.Add Carrots and Chicken to roux. Once mixture has thickened, stir in broth.

5.After it thickens again, add Gnocchi, Spinach, and seasonings. Simmer over med-low heat until heated through.

Serve as is. Pairs well with bread bowls.
