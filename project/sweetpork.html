Sweet Pork

Ingredients:

2 lbs Boneless Pork Roast

3 cans(4 1/2 c) Coke(not diet)

1/4 c Brown Sugar

dash Garlic Salt

1/4 c Water

1 4oz can Green Chilies

1 c Red Enchilada Sauce

1 c Brown Sugar

Instructions

1. Place pork in ziplock bag with 2 1/4 c Coke and 1/4 c Brown Sugar. Marinade overnight in fridge.

2. Drain marinade and put pork, 3/4 c Coke, Water, and Garlic Salt in crock pot(4 hours on high, 8 hours on low) or instant pot(1 hour on high pressure). Remove pork and drain any liquid. Shred pork.

3. Blend 3/4 c Coke, Chilies, Red Enchilada Sauce, and 1 c Brown Sugar until smooth. Add more sugar or Coke to achieve desired thickness.

4. Combine shredded pork and sauce in crockpot on low for 2 hours, or in instantpot on high for 5 minutes.

Serve as burros, enchiladas, on salad, or however your heart desires.
